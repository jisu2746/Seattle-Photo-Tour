Seattle Photo Spot Guide

Capture the magic of the Emerald City. Check off your favorites!

Pike Place Market

  • Location: Downtown Waterfront
  • Best Time: Early morning (to avoid crowds) or late afternoon
  • Mood: Bustling, Historic, Colorful, Local
💡 Photo Tips: Get a classic shot of the "Pike Place Market" neon sign. Inside, photograph the colorful flower stalls, the famous fish toss, or the quirky Gum Wall just downstairs in Post Alley.

The Space Needle

  • Location: Seattle Center
  • Best Time: Sunset or "Golden Hour"
  • Mood: Iconic, Futuristic, Panoramic, Urban
💡 Photo Tips: For the full structure, take photos from Seattle Center loop. If you go up, use the glass floors ("The Loupe") for dramatic top-down shots of the city below.

Kerry Park Viewpoint

  • Location: Queen Anne Hill
  • Best Time: Evening (for city lights) or clear days (for Mt. Rainier)
  • Mood: Romantic, Scenic, Classic, Skyline
💡 Photo Tips: This is the *most famous* view of Seattle. On a clear day, capture the skyline with the Space Needle perfectly placed in front of Mount Rainier. Great for night photography.
